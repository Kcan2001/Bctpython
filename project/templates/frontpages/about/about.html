About us:

At Black Crow Tours we focus on meeting fun, local people and seeing local culture while providing the best tour package value for people in our demographic:

Small group sizes allow for truly unique experiences.

Guides have the freedom to be themselves, for pure and honest tours.

Trips all include flights,transfers, bookings, accommodations, and much more

Each trip gives you the option of 'Short' or 'Regular', because we respect your time and this way most friends can also go; even those who might not be able to take off as much time... But don't worry if you're coming alone! Plenty of people do, and always leave with a ton of new friends!

We believe that life is too short to not do amazing things, and, as young adults, life is both an adventure and a party. This is why most trips also feature a local event you'll have an option to join; an event your guide personally enjoys.

Join us on our next adventure!


WHY 'BLACK CROW'?

The name "BlackCrow" was chosen because crows are one of the smartest birds on the planet. Crows are capable of building tools and working together to achieve goals.

They are found everywhere on earth, can eat anything, and are one of the few animals thriving with human contact.

Yet, for some reason, crows have a stigma of being grim. BlackCrow strives to be a tool for helping people to think differently about life. Where society sees something strange; we see intelligence, prosperity, and freedom.
