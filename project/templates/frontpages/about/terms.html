Automatic Payment Plan
All travelers are enrolled in Blackcrow's free, no interest Payment Plan. Our Payment Plan divides tour costs over time so payments are split in increments and deducted regularly directly from a checking account. Travelers can elect the payment plan option by choosing the deposit. Final payment will be due 60 days prior to departure.

Automatic Payment Plan Terms and Conditions
To qualify a minimum of two automated payments are required. Travelers who choose monthly payments will be billed on the 15th of each month. Should the final payment be returned or declined, travelers will automatically be withdrawn from the plan. After the final payment deadline you will be invoiced for any subsequent charges; payments will no longer be automatically deducted. Additional payments must be made by direct debit from your checking account.

Passports and Visas
It is the responsibility of each traveler to obtain a passport and any applicable visas for his or her tour prior to departure. Please be sure the passport expiration date is valid for at least six months after your travel ends. Certain countries require that your passport’s expiration date be valid for at least six months after your return, and should your passport fail to meet these standards the airline can deny your entry. Non-U.S. citizens will need to contact the embassies or consulates of their destination countries to ensure they meet specific entry requirements. Remember to check your itinerary carefully for all countries that you will visit or pass through, including transfers between airports in foreign countries and re-entry into the United States. Visit the U.S. Department of State at travel.state.gov for further information. If a traveler is unable to obtain a passport and/or visa and cannot travel, our Cancellation Policy will apply.
