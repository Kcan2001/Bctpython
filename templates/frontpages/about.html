About us

At Black Crow Tours we focus on meeting fun, local people and seeing local culture while providing the best tour package value for people in our demographic.

Small group sizes allow for truly unique experiences.

Guides have the freedom to be themselves, for pure and honest tours.

All trips include flights,transfers, bookings, Accommodations, and much more.

We offer ‘Short’ and ‘Regular’ versions of all our trips, because we respect your time and this way most friends can also go, even those who might not be able to take off as much time. Don't worry if you're coming alone! Plenty of people do, and they always leave with a ton of new friends!

We believe that life is too short to not do amazing things, and as young adults life is both an adventure and a party. This is why most trips also feature a local event you'll have an option to attend, an event your guide personally enjoys.

WHY 'BLACK CROW'?

The name "Black Crow" was chosen because crows are one of the smartest species of birds on the planet. Crows are capable of building tools and working together to achieve goals.

They are found everywhere on earth, can eat anything, and are one of the few animals thriving with human contact.

Yet, for some reason, crows have a stigma of being grim. Black Crow strives to be a tool for helping people think differently about life. Where society sees something strange, we see intelligence, prosperity, and freedom.
